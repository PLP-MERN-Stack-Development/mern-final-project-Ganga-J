About SDG 6

Understanding the global water crisis and our commitment to change

Clean Water Access

Ensure universal and equitable access to safe and affordable drinking water for all by 2030.

Sanitation & Hygiene

Achieve access to adequate and equitable sanitation and hygiene for all, ending open defecation.

Water Quality

Improve water quality by reducing pollution, eliminating dumping, and minimizing release of hazardous chemicals.

Water Efficiency

Substantially increase water-use efficiency across all sectors and ensure sustainable withdrawals.

Ecosystem Protection

Protect and restore water-related ecosystems, including mountains, forests, wetlands, and rivers.

International Cooperation

Support developing countries in water and sanitation-related activities and programs.

Water Conservation Tips

Simple changes that make a big difference

Shorter Showers

Reduce shower time by 2 minutes to save up to 40 liters of water per shower.

Save 40L/shower

Turn Off While Brushing

Turn off the tap while brushing teeth. This simple act saves up to 12 liters per minute.

Save 24L/day

Fix Leaky Toilets

A leaky toilet can waste up to 750 liters per day. Check and fix leaks promptly.

Save 750L/day

Full Dishwasher Loads

Only run the dishwasher when full. This maximizes water efficiency per dish.

Save 20L/load

Wash Produce in Bowl

Use a bowl to wash fruits and vegetables instead of running water.

Save 10L/wash

Boil Only What You Need

Only boil the amount of water you need for tea or cooking.

Save 5L/day

Water Plants Wisely

Water plants early morning or evening to reduce evaporation by up to 30%.

Save 30% water

Collect Rainwater

Install rain barrels to collect water for gardening and outdoor cleaning.

Save 5000L/year

Use a Bucket for Car

Wash your car with a bucket instead of a hose to save significant water.

Save 300L/wash

Full Laundry Loads

Wait for a full load before running the washing machine.

Save 50L/load

Reduce Meat Consumption

Producing 1kg of beef requires 15,000 liters of water. Consider plant-based alternatives.

Save 15000L/kg
